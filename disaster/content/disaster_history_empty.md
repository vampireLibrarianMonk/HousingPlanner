### Historical Disaster Declarations (County-Level)

This layer summarizes Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) disaster declarations affecting the county over time.
The data are county-level declaration records from FEMA's public disaster declarations API.
Use this to gauge regional exposure to hazards even if a specific address has no local overlay.
No records returned can mean limited declarations or a filtering mismatch.
